Who we are

Feeding the world Inc. is a Massachusetts-based not-for-profit organisation, focused on community-based initiatives and poverty reduction. We are particularly focused on supporting smallholder farmers in resource-limited settings.

Our Vison

To improve wellbeing and eradicate poverty and malnutrition through agricultural development in specified communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to conduct actionable research and develop programs to address challenges across the value chain for key agricultural commodities in resource-limited settings. Through our research, we identify crops that are central to the livelihood and wellbeing of smallholder farmers in target communities and characterize the failures in the value chain. We incubate and scale up solutions to the challenges and bottle-necks across the value chain.

Our Approach

Our unique approach focuses on combining ethnographic approaches to defining problems, and co-creating market-based solutions that have ecological validity. We develop customized tools to identify priority communities for our programming based on potential to benefit from interventions across the value chain, and readiness for intervention.

We apply focused ethnographic approaches including in-depth interviewing, participant observation and other approaches to identify the key failures in the value chain, from farm to fork. We work with smallholder farmers to incubate solutions ranging from provision of credit, introduction of improved inputs, support with mechanization, storage and processing facilities and facilitating access to the market. Our interventions are backed with robust monitoring and learning activities towards optimizing impact.

What we want to achieve

  • i. To enable farmers, improve their farm and non-farm incomes

  • ii. ii.To ensure food security, improved nutrition and poverty eradication.
