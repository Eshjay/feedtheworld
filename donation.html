Save the World from Hunger

With each donation:

We are focused on feeding the vulnerables in the communities. We are concerned about spate of disease and untimely death which might likely arise from poor feeding and malnourishment.
We embark on serious agricultural drive which covers the scope of food production, food processing and storage.

Feeding the World Inc organises free agricultural extension programme where we train the peasant and commercial farmers on improved agricultural practices. Our Agricultural Extension Programme stretches to the provision of improved viable seeds, genetically improved animals and above all the needed technologies to put them to work.

Our Donation Policy

All donations received by Feeding The World Inc. are used for purposes and expenses related to events and programmes for which they are intended and solicited. For general donations, Feeding The World Inc. may use your contribution wherever most needed

Your donations are highly secure with us

All credit card, paypal and personal profile information provided to Feeding The World through its Internet software partner is safe, faster, more secure way to donate online with, your payment is sent directly to the vulnerable like farmers and children.
