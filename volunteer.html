Get Involved in your area

Join us at one of our international locations and help prepare materials for distribution to communities in need.

Depending on the location and situation, tasks may include:

  • Reception and sorting of donated items.
  • Facilitating product distributions.
  • Assisting with special events like rallies, advocacy visits, International Day Celebrations, Research and etc.
  • Resource mobilization.
  • Planning conference, seminar, training and workshop.
Volunteer as an individual, or register for a group activity.
